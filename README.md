# Bitcoin Time-Series & Seasonality Analysis (2021–2025)

## 📊 Objective
To analyze Bitcoin price behavior using time-series and seasonality techniques in order to identify recurring patterns, profitability trends, and potential trading opportunities across different time frames (day, month, and year).

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Visualization)
- Excel / CSV dataset

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Source: Investing.com (BTC/USD Historical Data)

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 📅 Day of the Week
- Wednesday shows the highest average return (+0.42%)
- Thursday is the only day with a negative trend (-0.17%)
- Suggests potential "buy the dip" opportunity before weekend

---

### 📊 Win Rate Analysis
- Sunday has the highest win rate (~52.48%)
- Monday follows closely (~50.96%)
- Indicates higher probability of positive returns during weekends

---

### 📆 Monthly Seasonality
- June 2022 shows the worst drawdown (-37.32%)
- February 2024 shows strong recovery (+43.66%)
- Some months consistently show high volatility patterns

---

### 📈 Yearly Performance
- 2022 = Bear Market (Negative Year)
- 2023–2024 = Recovery & Growth Phase
- 2024 shows strongest performance among all years

---

## 📉 Business / Trading Interpretation
- Market exhibits clear cyclical behavior
- Short-term inefficiencies exist across weekdays
- Seasonality can support better timing strategies

---

## 💡 Strategy Recommendations
- Consider buying on Thursdays (historically weakest day)
- Focus on weekend trading opportunities (higher win rate)
- Monitor high-volatility months (e.g., June)
- Use yearly trend context before entering positions
